About Yorktown Poodles

At Yorktown Poodles, we are passionate about breeding exceptional Poodles that embody the breed's intelligence, elegance, and loving nature. Located in the heart of Virginia, our family-owned breeding program focuses on producing healthy, well-socialized puppies that make wonderful family companions.

Our commitment extends beyond breeding. We provide early socialization and ongoing support to ensure every puppy finds their perfect forever home. Each breeding dog is selected for temperament, health, and conformation to breed standards.

We specialize in Miniature and Toy Poodles in a variety of colors including cream, white, black, and apricot. Our puppies are raised in a loving home environment where they receive individual attention and care from day one.
